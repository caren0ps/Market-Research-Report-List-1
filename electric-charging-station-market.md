In contrast, wireless charging systems use electromagnetic fields to transfer energy between a charging pad and the vehicle, providing a more convenient, cable-free experience. Both systems cater to the growing demand for electric vehicle infrastructure, each with distinct advantages in terms of usability and technology integration.</p></p>
