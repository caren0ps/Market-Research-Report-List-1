<p><p>The automotive adaptive lighting market encompasses three main types: halogen, LED, and xenon lighting systems. Halogen lights are traditional, offering decent visibility at a low cost, but with limited efficiency and lifespan. LED lights provide superior brightness, energy efficiency, and longevity, making them increasingly popular in modern vehicles. Xenon (HID) lights offer brighter illumination than halogen, with a longer lifespan, yet they are more expensive and require specific housing. Together, these technologies enhance driving safety and visibility under varying conditions.</p></p>
