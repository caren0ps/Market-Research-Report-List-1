<p><p>The automotive tester market encompasses various types of equipment essential for vehicle performance evaluation. Chassis dynamometers assess the complete vehicle's power and torque output by simulating real-world driving conditions. Engine dynamometers focus on testing the engine alone, measuring performance characteristics like horsepower and efficiency. Vehicle emission test systems analyze exhaust emissions to ensure compliance with environmental regulations. Wheel alignment testers evaluate the alignment of a vehicle's wheels, crucial for ensuring optimal handling, tire wear, and safety.</p></p>
